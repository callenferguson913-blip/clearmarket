Why I built this

I'm a student at UNC Chapel Hill. A while back I decided I wanted to start investing — I knew it was important, I knew I wanted to grow my money, but every time I opened a brokerage app I had no idea what to actually buy. There was too much noise, too many options, and everything I read online felt like it was written for people who already knew what they were doing.

So I built ClearMarket. Every Monday morning it analyzes the market, looks at your portfolio and goals, and sends you a straightforward report — here's what's happening, here are your options, here's what I'd buy. No jargon. No overwhelm. Just a clear plan for your money that week.

If you're just getting started, this is the tool I wish I had.

— Callen, Founder
